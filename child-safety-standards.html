Notover has a zero-tolerance policy regarding child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE). We are committed to maintaining a safe environment and strictly prohibit any content, communication, or activity related to child sexual abuse material (CSAM).

1. Our Commitment to Child Safety

Notover is a couples connection app designed for adults to share moments together. We are deeply committed to:

  • Preventing the creation, sharing, or distribution of child sexual abuse material (CSAM)
  • Protecting minors from exploitation and abuse
  • Complying with all relevant child safety laws and regulations
  • Reporting suspected child abuse to appropriate authorities
  • Working with law enforcement and child safety organizations

2. Age Requirements

Minimum Age: Users must be 18 years or older to use Notover.

We do not knowingly allow minors to create accounts or use our services. Any accounts found to be created by or for minors will be immediately terminated.

3. Prohibited Content and Behavior

The following activities are strictly prohibited on Notover:

  • Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM): Any visual depiction of minors engaged in sexually explicit conduct
  • Child Exploitation: Any content that sexualizes, grooms, or exploits children
  • Child Solicitation: Any attempt to contact, groom, or solicit minors
  • Distribution: Sharing, uploading, or transmitting any CSAM content
  • Inappropriate Content: Any content depicting or promoting abuse, exploitation, or endangerment of children

4. Detection and Prevention Measures

Notover employs multiple safeguards to prevent CSAM and protect children:

4.1 Age Verification

  • Users must authenticate with Google Sign-In, providing age-verified accounts
  • Account creation requires an adult email address
  • We monitor for suspicious account patterns

4.2 Content Monitoring

  • User-generated content (drawings and canvas data) is monitored for policy violations
  • Automated systems flag potentially harmful content for review
  • Manual review processes for flagged content

4.3 User Controls

  • Users control who they pair with using secure pairing codes
  • No public profiles or open messaging to strangers
  • Users can unpair and block partners at any time

5. Reporting Mechanisms

5.1 In-App Reporting

Users can report safety concerns through:

  • Email: ahadnaseer47@gmail.com with subject "Safety Report - Urgent"
  • All reports are reviewed within 24 hours
  • Emergency reports are prioritized and handled immediately

5.2 What to Report

Please report any of the following:

  • Suspected child sexual abuse material (CSAM)
  • Users who appear to be minors
  • Grooming behavior or inappropriate solicitation
  • Content depicting child exploitation or endangerment
  • Suspicious account activity related to minors

5.3 Anonymous Reporting

Reports can be made anonymously. We protect the identity of reporters and take all reports seriously, regardless of the source.

6. Response and Action Protocol

When child safety concerns are reported or detected, we take immediate action:

6.1 Immediate Actions

  • Content Removal: Suspected CSAM is immediately removed and preserved as evidence
  • Account Suspension: Accounts involved are immediately suspended pending investigation
  • Access Block: Users are prevented from creating new accounts or accessing the service

6.2 Investigation

  • All reports are thoroughly investigated by our safety team
  • Evidence is preserved in accordance with legal requirements
  • We cooperate fully with law enforcement investigations

6.3 Permanent Removal

  • Users found in violation face permanent account termination
  • IP addresses and device identifiers are blocked
  • All user data related to violations is preserved for law enforcement

7. Reporting to Authorities

Notover complies with all legal reporting requirements and works with authorities to combat child exploitation:

7.1 National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC)

We report all instances of suspected CSAM to NCMEC's CyberTipline as required by US law (18 U.S.C. ¬ß 2258A).

  • CyberTipline: www.cybertipline.org
  • Hotline: 1-800-843-5678

7.2 Law Enforcement Cooperation

  • We cooperate fully with law enforcement investigations
  • We respond promptly to valid legal requests and subpoenas
  • We preserve evidence as required by law
  • We maintain relationships with local, national, and international law enforcement

7.3 International Reporting

For users outside the United States, we report to appropriate regional and national authorities:

  • Internet Watch Foundation (IWF): For UK and international reports
  • INHOPE: Network of hotlines in 47 countries
  • Local authorities: As required by regional laws

8. Privacy and Data Handling

In child safety investigations:

  • User privacy is balanced with child safety obligations
  • Evidence is securely stored and only shared with appropriate authorities
  • We comply with data protection laws while fulfilling safety obligations
  • Reporter identities are protected

9. Staff Training and Accountability
